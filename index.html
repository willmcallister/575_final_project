Madison Future Biking

Explore the Future of Biking in Madison!

This interactive map allows you to visualize how select bike infrastructure projects in Madison, WI plan to change current roads layouts, paths, and more. Using engineering drawings produced by the City of Madison and contractors, this map aims to simplify the process of understanding infrastructure projects without spending too much time attempting to understand complex engineering drawings.

You can use the project map to see exactly where new bike projects will be. The comparison map shows you an overview where current and future bike paths and trails are located. Use the slider bar to see the before and after and use the dropdown to select different path designations by the City.
